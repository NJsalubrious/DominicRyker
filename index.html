The Same Breath

Italy, 2019.

They tell the story of the convoy. How I saved a man from the wreckage, dragged him to the treeline. They call that heroism.

Then they tell the story of how I ended him an hour later because he hesitated. They call that murder.

It was the same action.

I removed a liability from a burning truck. Then I removed a liability from my organization. The moral difference exists only in your hesitation. I do not hesitate. That is why I am out here, and the system is back there, looking for a man who walked out the front door.
